About

This website was designed for anyone interested in looking for some new fun hikes to do, or to add to the collection from your own experiences for other people to enjoy as well.

When filling out the form to add your hike, there is an option to add the coordinates, to do this please visit Google Maps, find your location and click on the map. This will provide the coordinates for you to copy and add to the form in the correct format.
